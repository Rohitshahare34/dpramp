Refund Policy

Last Updated: {{ "now"|date:"F d, Y" }}

At DPRAMP-TECH SOLUTIONS LLP, we strive to provide high-quality educational services. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued.

1. Digital Study Materials

Non-Refundable Items:
  • PDF study materials and notes
  • Downloadable resources
  • Online course content
  • Digital templates and guides
Exceptions:
  • Technical issues preventing download (within 24 hours)
  • Incorrect or damaged files (verified within 48 hours)
  • Duplicate charges (verified immediately)

2. Educational Workshops

Refund Conditions:
  • 7+ days before workshop: Full refund minus 10% processing fee
  • 3-6 days before workshop: 50% refund
  • Less than 3 days before workshop: No refund
  • Workshop cancellation by us: Full refund
Rescheduling:
  • Workshop dates can be rescheduled up to 3 days before
  • Subject to availability of future sessions
  • No additional cost for first reschedule

3. Project Guidance Services

Refund Policy:
  • Before project commencement: 80% refund
  • After initial consultation: No refund
  • If service quality is disputed: Partial refund after review

4. Technical Services

Refund Conditions:
  • Service not delivered within agreed timeline: Full refund
  • Service quality issues: Partial refund after assessment
  • Client cancellation: No refund after work begins

5. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund:
  1. Send email to info@dpramp.com with subject "Refund Request"
  2. Include order details and reason for refund
  3. Provide supporting documentation if applicable
  4. Wait for our response within 5-7 business days
Refund Processing:
  • Approved refunds processed within 7-10 business days
  • Refunds issued to original payment method
  • Bank processing times may vary (3-7 additional days)
  • You will receive confirmation email once processed

6. Non-Refundable Situations

No refunds will be provided for:

  • Change of mind after digital delivery
  • Failure to attend scheduled workshops
  • Incomplete or incorrect information provided by client
  • Force majeure events
  • Services already rendered satisfactorily

7. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision:

  1. Contact our support team within 7 days of decision
  2. Provide additional evidence or clarification
  3. Request review by senior management
  4. Final decision will be communicated within 3 business days

8. Special Circumstances

We may consider exceptions for:

  • Medical emergencies (with documentation)
  • Technical issues on our end
  • Service delivery failures
  • Other exceptional circumstances at our discretion

9. Contact Information

For refund-related inquiries:

  • Email: info@dpramp.com
  • Phone: +91 9359919779
  • Support hours: Monday-Saturday, 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM
Important Notes:
  • This policy applies to all payments made through our website
  • Refund requests must be made within 30 days of service delivery
  • We reserve the right to modify this policy with prior notice
  • Final refund decisions are at the discretion of DPRAMP-TECH SOLUTIONS LLP
Our Commitment:

We are committed to fair and transparent refund practices. If you have any concerns about our services, please contact us immediately so we can address them appropriately.
